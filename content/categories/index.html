Notes

  1. If team members are not in the same category, then the team must enter in the highest category of the members.
  2. Entry fees do NOT apply for an on-line contest.
Category Description Entry Fee
School Team members must all attend a secondary school in New Zealand. This includes a home school. $30
Tertiary Junior Usually first year university students. Have not started any second year papers. $75
Tertiary Intermediate Usually second year university students. Have not started any third year papers. $75
Tertiary Open University students who do not qualify for the previous 2 categories. $75
Open Usually professional programmers. $120
